RETRO GAMING ZONE

Every corner is designed to transport you back to the legendary 8-bit era, complete with CRT monitors, neon lights, and nostalgic soundtracks that'll make you feel like you've entered a time machine to the 1980s.

Our master baristas are true pixel artists, crafting each drink with precision and creativity that rivals the greatest game developers. From our signature 8-bit latte art featuring Mario, Sonic, and retro game sprites, to our specially formulated "Power-Up" blends that boost your energy levels like collecting a mushroom in Super Mario Bros. PIXEL ART ATMOSPHERE
Immerse yourself in a carefully curated environment where every detail pays homage to gaming history.
